Our Committees

Technical Committee

This committee involves people who are good in coding, designing and handling the official website, Instagram, Twitter, Facebook page and really good with computers. They are also responsible for hosting all the technical events.

Designing Committee

This committee involves people who are good in designing and editing of pictures irrespective of which software they work on. They are responsible for designing of posters, banners of the various events and fest held throughout the year.

Editorial Committee

This committee includes people who are well versed with words and good in article writing, describing the various events held, designing and formatting of the magazine, etc. They are committed to offering readers a mix of newsworthy, engaging, and thought-provoking articles about the Club.

Photography Committee

This committee involves people who are responsible for capturing and archiving photographic memories of various events of our club. This involves people who are good with creativity and have to click photographs of the various events held from time to time.

Event Committee

This committee involves people who heave a creative mind and good in management. They are responsible for bringing up new ideas for new events. They will also be looking after all the requirements for an event to be successful. They are responsible for the overall development and management of the Event including recruiting exhibitors and sponsors, and promotions.

Public Relation Committee

This committee people who are good in making new connections. They are responsible for bringing sponsorships for the various events and the promotions of the events happening in the college. They will also be responsible for helping out others with their doubts about our club.
