About us

By now you must be aware of what this club has to offer and how we conduct ourselves. But you don’t really know what UCD is. Let’s understand it together and have a tour of how this club began and what is the main idea behind. Freedom and zeal, I think these are the most precise words that define the motivation to start this club. As we come to college, every student has the same idea in mind: a new life and new possibilities. Well, that all changes when you become the part of this competitive and hectic regime that’s called student life. Classes, exams, practicals, assignments etc. they can change a person to his/her core. Following this schedule day by day, makes us forget what we are, and at the end of the day it has so little to offer.

So, what should you do in a situation like this? Well for us, we started a club. A club that was free of any limitations and never confined on ideas. A club where everyone can be themselves without worrying of the consequences. A club where you and I can practice and create that which is important to us. We named it the UNITED CLUB OF DEVELOPERS. Don’t get the wrong idea, this club is not a technical club. The beauty of UCD is that it can become anything based on your needs. We believe that diversity in ideas is of the utmost importance, therefore we decided that we as a community will always encourage a healthy flow of ideas. Also, we believe that every individual is uniquely talented may it be in studies, dance, music etc. therefore we aim to create an environment where your talent can grow without any limitations.
